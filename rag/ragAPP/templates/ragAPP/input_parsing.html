Why Document Parsing is Essential in RAG

Document parsing is the critical first step in any RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline. Here's why it matters:

Text Extraction: Converts binary formats (PDF) into readable text that AI models can process.
Noise Removal: Eliminates formatting artifacts, extra spaces, and unwanted characters.
Structure Preservation: Maintains meaningful text structure while fixing broken words and spacing.
Quality Preparation: Ensures clean input for accurate chunking and embedding generation.
